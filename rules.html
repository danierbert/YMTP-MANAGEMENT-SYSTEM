YMTP MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Rules and Regulations

Welcome to the YMTP Management System. Please adhere to the following rules and regulations:


For Officers:

  1. Know your Role.
    • President/Chairperson - Leads the team and meetings
    • Vice President - Supports the President
    • Secretary - Writes minutes and sends updates
    • Treasurer - Manages money and budgets
    • Auditor - Checks if funds are used properly
    • Public Relations Officers (P.R.O.) - Handles announcements and promotions
    • Board of Directors (B.O.D.) - Support and leading the group
    • Sgt. at Arms - Keeps peace & order during meetings and activities
    • Book keeper - Organizes and keeps all receipts, vouchers, and financial documents
  2. Show Up
    • Attend all Meetings (regular or Special)
    • Give a valid Reason if you can't attend
    • 3 unexcused absences: possible replacement
  3. Decide Together
    • Decisions are made through voting
    • Everyone has a voice - speak respectfully
    • Keep sensitive topics confidential
  4. Be a good leader
    • Show respect, honesty, and teamwork
    • Represent the organzation well (in school, online, and outside)
    • No drama, bullying, or toxic behavior
  5. Do your job right
    • Submit Reports on time
    • Use money and resources responsibly
    • Can be removed for not doing your duties or misuse of funds


For All:

  • Respect all members and officers.
  • Maintain a positive attitude and contribute to discussions.
  • Report any violations or issues to the officers immediately.
  • Participate in events and activities regularly.
  • Show up! Attend all meetings - regular or special
  • 3 unexcused absences may result to possible replacement - For officers
  • Butaw - 20 Pesos every Meeting

Penalty for every late and absences

  • Late- 5mins: 5 pesos, 10 mins: 10 Pesos
  • Absent - Without valid reason/No excuse: Warning

Every members and officers of YMTP org. Must submit the corresponding requirements:

  1. PSA Birth Certificate
  2. ID
  3. Formal Picture white Background


Failure to comply with these rules may result in warnings or disciplinary actions.
